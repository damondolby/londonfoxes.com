Background

Leicester City Supporters' Club (London Branch) was founded prior to the 1977-78 season and covers primarily London and its bordering counties.

Travel

The core activity of the club used to be centred around travelling together to home and away matches. However for most journeys by train now group travel is less of a saving so most supporters are now making their own arrangements. However if you would like to meet to travel together please get in touch.

Social

The club has a darts team and we play against London Branches of other Supporters' Clubs. In 2014-15 we won the cup and were second in the league.
